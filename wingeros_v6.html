Non-negotiables
The rules that don't move
Every week, regardless of how you feel or how busy work is.
1
8 hours sleep minimum. Below this, everything else does less for you.
2
3L water daily, 4L+ on training days. 2% dehydration cuts sprint performance 5–10%.
3
145g protein every day. Including rest days.
4
Warm up every session. 10 minutes minimum. Cold sprints = hamstring tears.
5
One full rest day a week. Sunday. Recovery is when adaptation happens.
6
No alcohol weeks 1–10. Blunts protein synthesis 24h and wrecks REM sleep.
7
Never grind reps. Leave 1–2 in the tank on every main lift.
8
Don't double up missed sessions. Pick up where the plan says.
